About Épure Living

Simplicity is a
choice worth making.

Épure Living was born from a belief that our surroundings shape how we feel. When our spaces are cluttered, our minds follow. When our wardrobe is overwhelming, getting dressed becomes a burden instead of a joy.

I'm Nika, and I guide individuals and families through the process of thoughtfully editing their homes and wardrobes — creating environments that are beautiful, functional, and genuinely theirs.

"A clear space, a clear mind."

What I Offer

01

Home Decluttering

A room-by-room process to sort, simplify, and let go. We work together to decide what earns a place in your home — keeping only what serves you.

02

Space Organisation

After decluttering, we create smart, intuitive systems for storage and organisation — so everything has a home and stays that way.

03

Capsule Wardrobe

Edit your closet down to a curated, versatile collection you truly love. Wake up every morning knowing exactly what to wear — and feeling great in it.

How it works

A gentle, guided process

I

Discovery Call

We start with a free 30-minute conversation to understand your goals, challenges, and vision for your space.

II

Home Visit

I come to you for an in-person session, working side by side through your space or wardrobe at your own pace.

III

Edit & Organise

Together we sort, decide, and create beautiful functional systems tailored to your lifestyle and daily rhythms.

IV

Follow-Up

A check-in to ensure your new systems are working and to answer any questions that arise along the way.
